Welcome to El Agave

El Agave serves authentic Mexican cuisine in a vibrant, festive atmosphere with friendly service, flavorful dishes, and refreshing drinks that keep guests coming back.

Located in McDonough, GA, we pride ourselves on using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes to bring you the true taste of Mexico.

Whether you're joining us for a casual dinner, celebrating a special occasion, or ordering catering for your next event, we're committed to providing an exceptional dining experience.

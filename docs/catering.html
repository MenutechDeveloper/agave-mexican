Catering FAQs

How much notice do you need?

We can often accommodate within 48–72 hours. Larger events appreciate 5–7 days' notice.

Do you offer delivery and setup?

Yes — we deliver, label items, and provide serving utensils. Chafers & fuel available upon request.

Can you handle dietary requests?

Absolutely. Let us know about vegetarian, vegan, or gluten‑friendly needs in the form.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve McDonough and surrounding areas. Include your venue address in the form for an exact delivery quote.
